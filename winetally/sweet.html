Wine Tally

Sweet wines

Sweet wines have the higher levels of residual sugar. During fermentation the sugar is converted to alcohol by yeast. If fermentation is halted prior to complete conversion from sugar to alcohol, some of the sugar will reside in the wine, hence the term "residual sugar". Sweet wines are very approachable and work well with spicy dishes.

Works well with

Sweet and spicy dishes, such as Thai, Indian, or Chinese cuisine; Oily fish, chicken. Citrus-based dishes, pungeant cheeses, and desserts.
