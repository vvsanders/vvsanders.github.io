Wine Tally

Rose wines

To make rosé, red grapes are lightly crushed and left to macerate with their red skins for some time (which will determine the color of the wine). Rosé is a happy medium between reds and whites, but unlike most wines, it doesn't improve over the years. Rosé wines can be made from any varietals.

Works well with

Spicy dishes; Mexican, Moroccan, Indian, and Mediterranean cuisines. Fried chicken, roast pork.

Wine Suggestions

  • Garnacha Rosé
  • Sangiovese Rosé
  • Cotes du Rhone
  • Pinot Noir Rosé
  • Provence Rosé
