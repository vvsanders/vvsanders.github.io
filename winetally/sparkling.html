Wine Tally

Sparkling wines

Sparkling wine contains significant levels of carbon dioxide in it, making it fizzy. The carbonation is created using one of two methods: the Champagne method or the Charmat-Martinotti method, the difference being the length of the process, and ultimately the sweetness and taste of the wine. Sparkling wines have four different levels of sweetness, from extra-brut to demi-sec.

Works well with

Salty foods; Fries, Anchovies, Sushi, Caviar, Fish, Shellfish, Poultry. Desserts such as fruit tarts.
