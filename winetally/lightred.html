Wine Tally

Light red wines

Light-bodied red wines are vastly appreciated for their versatility and delicacy. As the products of thin-skinned grapes, they are known for having lighter tannin, bright acidity and slightly lower alcohol. Light red wines typically have subtle aromas, ranging from red-fruits and flowers to, surprisingly, more smokey flavors.

Works well with

Risotto, Creamy pasta, poultry, mushrooms. French cuisine.
