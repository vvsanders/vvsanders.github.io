Wine Tally

Rich white wines

Rich whites are knows for their buttery and luxurious texture. They inherit nutty, vanilla, and spice notes from oak-barrel aging. Many full-bodied white wines will age up to 10 years, although most are in their prime at around 3-4 years. Their fullness makes them pair ideally with rich and creamy dishes.

Works well with

Cream-based dishes and French Cuisine; soups, pasta, quiche, lush sauces. Poultry and fatty fish , crab and lobster. Soft cheeses
