Dessert wines

Dessert wines are essentially sweet wines, produced with very sweet wine grapes. In order to make them sweet, the fermentation is stopped before the yeast turns all the natural grape sugar into alcohol. The fermentation is usually stopped by super-cooling the wine or adding brandy to it. Dessert wines are meant to be enjoyed in small glasses.

Works well with

Soft cheeses, fruits, and desserts; cookies, ice cream, cake, chocolate.

Wine Suggestions

  • Sherry
  • Port
  • Madeira
  • Muscat
  • Late Harvest Wines
