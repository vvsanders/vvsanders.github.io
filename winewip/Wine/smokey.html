Wine Tally

Smokey wines

def; a wine that smells like a camp fire.

The subtle aroma of smoked-wood, which is often a result of the wooden vats in which the wine is stored during production.

Works well with

Salty foods; Fries, Anchovies, Sushi, Caviar, Fish, Shellfish, Poultry.

Wine Suggestions

  • Prosecco
  • Cremant
  • Moscato
  • Cava
  • Moscato
